About Doggy Dice

Doggy Dice is a dog-themed dice game with adorable canine companions, Brody and Ollie. Watch them compete to fetch the most dice when you roll with charming animations and graphics.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Doggy Dice?

Doggy Dice is a fun and easy-to-play dice game. Roll the dice and watch Brody and Ollie fetch them for you. Score points based on the dice combinations and compete to get the highest score!

How can I turn off the sound effects?

You can turn off the sound effects in the game settings. Go to the settings menu and toggle the sound effects option.
